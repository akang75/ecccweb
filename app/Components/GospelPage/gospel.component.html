Gospel Events

Praise the Lord. Many brothers and sisters at ECCC, by the calling of God, actively participate in the missionary work all over the world. Our local gospel ministry includes our bi-monthly Gospel Nights and Seattle Gospel Center. In addition, we have established overseas missionary fields in the Far East, South America, Taiwan, Cambodia, and India.

Chinatown

感谢主,东区基督教会的弟兄姐妹在主的呼召下积极参与世界各地的宣教事工.除了藉在西雅图的福音中心和教会举办的福音晚会来推展本地的福音工作.并在海外建立了各个工场.如远东,巴西,印度,台湾,柬埔寨等地方.

唐人街布道
